About

CRCE Formula Racing team is Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering’s Formula SAE Team.

A mutual passion for automobiles and a zeal to learn formed the essence of CRCE Formula Racing. Thirty Fr. CRCE students from a variety of engineering disciplines make up the 2018 - 19 CRCE Formula Racing team. Each student brings instrumental skills to the team, the union between the diverse skill and strong team work forms the core of our team.

Our Title Partner

Bank of Baroda serves over 82 Million Customers across 22 Countries. Its tagline 'India's International Bank' speaks volumes for the companies outlook

Bank of Baroda started its overseas journey by opening its first branch way back in 1953 in Mombassa, Kenya. Since then the Bank has come a long way in expanding its international network to serve NRls/PIOs, Indian Corporates around the world and to meet the banking requirements of the local population in the country of operation. The Bank has transformed into 'India's International Bank.'
